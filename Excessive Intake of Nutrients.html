Excessive Intake of Nutrients – Overnutrition

11th–12th grade • Definition & basics • Complete excess table (macros + micros) • One excess‑mechanism diagram • Printable

1) What is Overnutrition?

When nutrients are taken in excess of body needs, they are stored or accumulate → may lead to diseases and toxicity.

  • Just like deficiency, excess can harm health.
  • Common outcomes: Obesity, Hypertension, Vitamin toxicity.

✅ Quick Recap for Students

  • Excess intake can be as harmful as deficiency.
  • Major risks: Obesity, Diabetes, Hypertension, Heart disease, Kidney problems, Vitamin/Mineral toxicities.
  • Prevention = Balanced diet — not too little & not too much.
