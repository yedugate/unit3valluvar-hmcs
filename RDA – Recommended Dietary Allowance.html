RDA – Recommended Dietary Allowance

11th–12th grade • Definition & simple importance • Complete RDA table (macros + micros) • Diagram linking RDA → balanced diet → health • Printable

1) Definition

RDA is the average daily intake of nutrients required to meet the needs of a healthy person.

  • Set by nutrition experts (e.g., ICMR, WHO).
  • Varies with age, sex, physical activity, and health condition.

✅ Quick Recap for Students

  • RDA = safe daily intake levels for nutrients.
  • Prevents both deficiency and excess.
  • Values differ for children, adults, pregnancy, elderly.
  • Balanced diet = meeting RDA daily.
