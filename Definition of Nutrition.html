Definition of Nutrition

Student‑friendly quick notes with a mini diagram & recap

Nutrition is the science of food and how the body uses it to keep us alive and healthy.

  • ✓
    Energy production — to work, play, study, exercise.
  • ✓
    Growth & development — bones, muscles, organs.
  • ✓
    Repair & maintenance — healing cuts, replacing old cells.
  • ✓
    Regulation of body functions — hormones, immunity, enzymes.

In biology, nutrition moves through steps: ingestion → digestion → absorption → assimilation → egestion.
