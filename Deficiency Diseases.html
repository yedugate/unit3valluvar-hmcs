1) What are Deficiency Diseases?

Deficiency diseases occur when the body does not get enough of a particular nutrient for a long time.

  • Affect growth, energy, immunity, and normal functioning.
  • Can be prevented by eating a balanced diet.

✅ Quick Recap for Students

  • Deficiency diseases are caused by lack of nutrients.
  • Carbs/Proteins/Fats → affect energy & growth.
  • Minerals/Vitamins → affect blood, bones, skin, nerves.
  • Balanced diet and food variety are the best prevention.
